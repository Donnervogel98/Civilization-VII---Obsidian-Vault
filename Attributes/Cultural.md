#### Civics
Cultural Time-Tested Civilizations gain access to the below Traditions through their unique Civic Trees
- Antiquity: **Enlightened Rule**
	- +15% towards constructing Wonders
- Exploration: **Classical Revival**
	- Buildings gain a +1 Adjacency for Wonders
- Modern: **Romanticism**
	- +2 Culture on Appealing Terrain

#### Diplomacy
Cultural Leaders gain access to the below diplomatic actions
- Endeavor: **Cultural Exchange**
	- Accepted
		- You gain +4 Culture per Age, and the receiver gains +2 Culture per Age
	- Supported
		- Both Leaders gain +6 Culture per Age
- Sanction: **Hinder Civic Study**
	- Target loses 10% of their Culture per turn

#### Legend Path Unlocks
Cultural Leaders gain access to the below upon reaching the appropriate level in their [[Legend Paths|Legend Path]]
- **Attribute Node (Lvl 3 or Lvl 7)**
	- +15% Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders with Great Work slots
- **Dedication (Lvl 4 & Lvl 8)**
	- +15% Culture, -10% Science
