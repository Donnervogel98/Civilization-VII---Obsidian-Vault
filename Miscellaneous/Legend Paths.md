Completing Challenges while playing the game will unlock various Mementos, Cosmetics, Legacy Cards, and Attribute Nodes for use in future games


## Leader Paths

Each Leader has their own Legend Path and Challenges, with unlocks listed below. Mementos are unique to each Leader and are made available for use on all Leaders once unlocked. Conversely, Attribute Nodes and Legacy Cards are determined by a Leader's Attributes, and must be unlocked individually for each Leader.

- Lvl 2: Unique Memento
- Lvl 3: Attribute Node for first Attribute
	- [[Cultural]]: +15% Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders with Great Work slots
	- [[Diplomatic]]: +25% Relationship from starting or supporting Endeavors. -25% Relationship from starting Sanctions or rejecting Endeavors
	- [[Economic]]: +25% Gold from Trade and Treasure Fleets
	- [[Expansionist]]: +50% Gold towards converting a Town to a City
	- [[Militaristic]]: +5 Combat Strength for all Units against Districts
	- [[Scientific]]: +1 to the adjacencies of standard Gold, Production, and Science Buildings
- Lvl 4: Legacy Cards for the Exploration Age
	- [[Cultural]]: +15% Culture, -10% Science
	- [[Diplomatic]]: +15% Influence, -2 Combat Strength for Naval (Exp.) or Air (Mod.) Units
	- [[Economic]]: +15% Gold, -10% Food
	- [[Expansionist]]: +15% Food, -10% Gold
	- [[Militaristic]]: +3 Combat Strength towards Naval (Exp.) or Air (Mod.) Units, -10% Influence
	- [[Scientific]]: +15% Science, -10% Culture
- Lvl 5: Unique Memento
- Lvl 6: Cosmetics: Badge and Banner
- Lvl 7: Attribute Node for second Attribute
- Lvl 8: Legacy Cards (see Lvl 4) for the Modern Age
- Lvl 9: Unique Memento
- Lvl 10: Cosmetics: Badge (Portrait) and Title


## Foundation Path

Completing Foundation (account-wide) Challenges will contribute to your Foundation Level, granting various bonuses

##### Mementos
Mementos are items that can be equipped to your Leader when starting a game or transitioning between Ages to provide them with various benefits. Any Memento, once unlocked, can be equipped to any Leader. You unlock a Memento Slot at Foundation Level 2, and a second slot at Level 5. Mementos unlocked via the Foundation Path are listed below.
- Lvl 2: ***Complaint to Ea-nasir***
	- +1 Economic Attribute Point
- Lvl 2: ***Inscribed Sling Bullet***
	- +1 Military Attribute Point
- Lvl 3: ***Groma***
	- +1 Expansionist Attribute Point
- Lvl 3: ***Treaty of Kadesh***
	- +1 Diplomatic Attribute Point
- Lvl 4: ***Antikythera Mechanism***
	- +1 Scientific Attribute Point
- Lvl 4: ***Shakokidogu***
	- +1 Cultural Attribute Point
- Lvl 5: ***Sword of Brennus***
	- +400 Gold for every Settlement returned in a Peace Deal
- Lvl 6: ***Lydian Lion***
	- +200 Gold per Age at the start of an Age
- Lvl 7: ***Imago Mundi***
	- +2 Sight instead of +1 for Scout's Search & Lookout abilities
- Lvl 8: ***Corpus Juris Civilis***
	- Start with one extra Social Policy Slot
- Lvl 9: ***The Travels of Marco Polo***
	- +50 Gold for every 100 Tiles Explored by your Units
- Lvl 14: ***Equestrian Figure***
	- +50% Reduction in Commander Recovery Time
- Lvl 19: ***Pochteca Backpack***
	- +5 range for all Trade Routes
- Lvl 24: Colada & Tizona
	- +2 Combat Strength for Units adjacent to 2 or more enemy Units
- Lvl 29: ***Shisa Necklace***
	- Gain 50 Influence per Age when you become Suzerain of a City-State
- Lvl 34: ***Agincourt Arrowhead***
	- +1 Movement for Ranged Units
- Lvl 39: ***Garuda Statue***
	- +1 Population in your smallest Settlement every time you start a Celebration
- Lvl 44: ***The Art of War***
	- +10% Commander Experience; +50% when within the Command Radius of a higher level Commander
- Lvl 49: ***Royal Game of Ur***
	- +10% Science when outpaced in Tech by half of Civs; +10% Culture when outpaced in Civics by half of Civs
