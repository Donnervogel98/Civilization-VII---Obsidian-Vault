## Unique Ability
##### *Bokh*
- When you capture an opposing Settlement, gain the strongest Cavalry Unit you can currently train
- -50% Production towards training Settlers
- [Ant] Chariots are unlocked with Agriculture
- [Mod] +3 Settlement Limit in Advanced Starts

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Improvement: *Örtöö*
- +5 Gold
- Restores Movement to your Units at its location
- Cannot be placed on Rough, River, or a tile with a Feature

## Unique Units
##### Ranged Unit: *Keshig*
- Has +1 Movement and heals 15 HP after defeating an enemy Unit
- Counts as a Cavalry Unit
##### Army Commander: *Noyan*
- +50% Combat Strength from Flanking for Cavalry Units in the Command Radius
- Has additional Movement
- Can move after attacking

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Origins*
- Tradition: **Baghatur I**
	- +3 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Foundation*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Expansionist|Fractal Cities]] and [[Militaristic|Warrior Class]] 
- +2 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Jarugachi I**
	- +1 Influence and Production on Horse Resources, doubled in Captured Settlements from any Age

## Civics – Exploration
