>*Like the monsoon rains, the Khmer give life to the land, and like the monsoon floods, they conquer what they will. The great mandala of Kambuja, the Khmer Empire, whirls with divine grace, and its power flows like water, a sanctuary against the burning sun. Stand on elephant-back, and let the waters rise. They will not touch you.*

## Unique Ability
##### *Ksekam Chamnon*
- Districts on Rivers do not remove the natural yield of the tile
- +50% Growth Rate in the Capital, but -5/-10/-15 Happiness for every other City

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Improvement: *Baray*
- +3 Food
- +1 Food on all Floodplains in this Settlement
- Improvements, Buildings, and Districts in this Settlement do not get pillaged by floods
- Must be placed on Flat Terrain
- One per Settlement

## Unique Units
##### Cavalry Unit: *Yuthahathi*
- Higher Combat Strength, but slower Movement
- Immune to flood damage
##### Merchant: *Vaishya*
- Immune to flood damage
- Ignores Movement penalties from Wet Terrain

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Mousong*
- Improvement: **Baray**
- Unlocks Merchants
- Tradition: **Pithi Chrat I**
	- +1 Food on Floodplains in Towns, but -5 Food in Cities other than your Capital
##### *Amnach*
- Tradition: **Bon Om Touk**
	- Science Buildings in Cities gain an adjacency for Floodplains
	- +3 Great Work slots on the Palace
- Wonder: **Angkor Wat**
##### *Chakravarti*
- Tradition: **Kambu-Mera I**
	- +50% Food and Happiness towards maintaining Specialists
- +1 Tradition slot

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Renaissance*
- Tradition: **Pithi Chrat II**
	- +2 Food on Floodplains in Towns, but -10 Food in Cities other than your Capital
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Hierarchy*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Expansionist|Municipal Charters]] and [[Scientific|Alchemy]]
- +1 Specialist Limit in the Capital for this Age
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Varna I**
	- +1 Gold on Specialists

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Kambu-Mera II**
	- +100% Food and Happiness towards maintaining Specialists
	- +1 Specialist Limit in the Capital
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Expansionist|Developmentalism]] and [[Scientific|Location Theory]]
- +1 Specialist Limit in the Capital for this Age
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Varna II**
	- +1 Gold and Science on Specialists

## Associated Wonder
##### *Angkor Wat*
- +3 Happiness
- +1 Specialist Limit in this Settlement
- Must be placed adjacent to a River

## Starting Biases
- Floodplains
- Tropical
