[[Cultural]], [[Expansionist]]

>*First among equals, Augustus Caesar claims his right. You, who never call yourself Emperor, are revered by your people as a god. The world now is disorder; you will give it form. The world is leaderless; you will provide it direction. Set the laurels on your head and issue your commands – lead your people to glory, and your name will become synonymous with rule.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Imperium Maius*
- +2 Production in the Capital for every Town
- Can purchase Culture Buildings in Towns with any Focus
- +15% Gold towards purchasing Buildings in Towns; doubled if that Building is present in your Capital

## Agenda
##### *Restitutor Orbis*
- ( - - ) for each Town in other Leaders' empires
- ( + + ) for each City (excluding the Capital) in other Leaders' empires

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Carthage]], [[Egypt]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Bulgaria]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Corona Civica***
	- +1 Settlement Limit each Age, but +50% Convert to City cost
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Breastplate***
	- +2 Food per Age in Towns
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Clipeus Virtutis***
	- +1 Production in the Capital for every Town
- Lvl 10 Title: **First Citizen**
