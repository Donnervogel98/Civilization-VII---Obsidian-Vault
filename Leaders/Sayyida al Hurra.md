## Leader Bonus
##### *Hakima Tatwan*
- Receive the strongest Naval Unit every time you succeed in an Espionage Action, if you can place a Naval Unit
- +2 Culture and +1 Influence per Age for every Naval Unit stationed on a District
- No Influence penalty for your Espionage being revealed
- Opponents cannot use Counter Spy against you

## Agenda
##### *Wattasid Dynasty*
- ( - - ) with Leaders who have the most Naval Units on the map
- ( + ) with Leaders who have the least Naval Units on the map

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Nimcha***
	- -1 Gold Maintenance on Naval Units
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Mithqāl***

- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Gazelle Vase***
	- Gain 25 Culture per Age for every Diplomatic Action accepted by another Leader
- Lvl 10 Title: **Hakima Tatwan**
