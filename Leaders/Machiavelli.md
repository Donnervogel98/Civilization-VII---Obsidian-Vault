>*A spider in his web, or a writer at his desk, you, Niccolo Machiavelli, see the world as it is, untempered by convention or sentimentality. To succeed in this world requires unconscionable acts, and it is a brave heart that can be made a stone. The time has come to set your plans in motion.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Il Principe*
- +3 Influence per Age
- Gains 50 Gold per Age when your Diplomatic Action proposals are accepted, or 100 Gold when they are rejected
- Ignores Relationship requirements for declaring Formal Wars
- Can Levy Troops from City-States you are not Suzerain of

## Agenda
##### *The Spider*
- ( + + ) for each war a Leader has if they are not at war with Machiavelli

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]], [[Silla Korea]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Russia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Discorsi sopra Livio***
	- Costs opponents 50% more Influence to reject your Diplomatic Actions
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Dell'Arte della Guerra***
	- +3 Gold per Sanction you have active per Age
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Il Principe***
	- Declaring a Formal War decreases relationship with all untargeted Civilizations by 25
- Lvl 10 Title: **The Prince**
