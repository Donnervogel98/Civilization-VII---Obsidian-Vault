Benjamin Franklin, bring your sound advice, and let us ensure that the sun rises on your people.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *The First American*
- +1 Science per Age on Production and Science Buildings in Cities
- +5% Production towards constructing Buildings for active Endeavors you have started or supported
- +2 Science per Age from active Endeavors you started or supported
- Can have two Endeavors of the same type active at a time

## Agenda
##### *Civic Virtue*
- ( + + + ) to Leaders with the same Government
- ( - - - ) to Leaders with a different Government

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[America]], [[French Empire]], [[Great Britain]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Bifocals***
	- Gain 25 Influence per Age after researching a Technology
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Kite & Key***
	- +10% Science towards researching Tech Masteries
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Glass Armonica***
	- +15 Science and Happiness per Age when in an Alliance for you and your ally
- Lvl 10 Title: **First American**
