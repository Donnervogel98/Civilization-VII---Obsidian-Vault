>*With a cry of defiance, you stand against those who would impose their will. Though your enemies, Trung Trac, may be many, you will make them pay for each step they take in your lands. Raise your cry, and call upon your people to stand with you. The sky darkens.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Hai Bà Trưng*
- Gain the Unique Army Commander **[[Trung Nhi]]** after producing your first Land Military Unit of the Age; **[[Trung Nhi]]** persists across Age Transitions, and begins with one Promotion per Age in Advanced Starts, and has a unique Promotion Tree
- +20% Commander experience
- +10% Science in Cities in Tropical, doubled while in a Formal War you declare

## Agenda
##### *Van Minh*
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the greatest number of total Commander Promotions, or ( - ) to each if there is a tie
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the fewest total Commander Promotions, or ( + ) to each if there is a tie

## Starting Biases
- Vegetated
- Tropical

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Khmer]], [[Maurya]]
- Exploration: [[Chola]], [[Đại Việt]], [[Majapahit]]
- Modern: [[Meiji Japan]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Kiem***
	- +2 Science for each Army Commander Promotion
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Ankus***
	- +1 War Support on all wars
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Dong Son Drum***
	- Declaring a Formal War against another Civilization grants a Tech Boost
- Lvl 10 Title: **Rebel Queen**
