## Leader Bonus
##### *Blackbeard*
- All Naval Units gain the Pirate Ability, allowing them to cross borders of other civilizations, Plunder Trade Routes from non-Allied Civilizations, and attack Units of non-Allied civilizations without declaring War
- Defeating any Naval Unit with your own Naval Units provides Gold equal to 50% of its Combat Strength and captures that Unit under your control
- +1 Gold Maintenance for Naval Units

## Agenda
##### *Queen Anne's Revenge*
- ( + ) with Leaders that build Naval Units
- ( - ) with Leaders that do not build Naval Units

- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Figurehead of the Queen Anne's Revenge***
