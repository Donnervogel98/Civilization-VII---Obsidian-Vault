## Leader Bonus
##### *Combahee Raid*
- +100% Influence towards initiating Espionage Actions; gain a Migrant in the Capital whenever you complete an Espionage Action without being detected
- Gain 5 War Support on all wars declared against you
- Vegetated Terrain does not end Unit Movement

## Agenda
##### *Veracity*
- ( + + ) for each Formal War a Leader has declared
- ( - - ) for each Surprise War a Leader has declared

## Starting Biases
- Vegetated

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Aksum]], [[Egypt]]
- Exploration: [[Inca]], [[Songhai]]
- Modern: [[America]], [[Buganda]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Lantern***
	- Gain a Migrant in the Capital whenever you complete an undetected Espionage Action
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Satchel***
	- If a Settler is defeated, it respawns in your Capital after 3 turns (on Standard Speed)
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Sidearm***
	- +3 Combat Strength to your units below 50% health
- Lvl 10 Title: **Conductor**
