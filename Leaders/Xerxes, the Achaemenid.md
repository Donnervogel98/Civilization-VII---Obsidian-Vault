*Alternate Persona of [[Xerxes, King of Kings]]*
*Available with the [[Deluxe Content Pack]] DLC*

[[Cultural]], [[Economic]]

>*Shah of shahs, you bring the fire of ambition to the world. Its light glitters off of the gold in your throne room – and the bronze blades of your warriors. You conceive of a new world, one of wonders rising from the wilderness, of an empire that defines time, and one that will never forget your name – Xerxes.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Silk Road*
- +1 Trade Route Limit with all other Leaders
- +10 Trade Range
- Gain 50 Culture and 100 Gold per Age when you create a Trade Route or Road
- +1 Culture and Gold per Age on Unique Buildings and Improvements

## Agenda
##### *Lord of Coin*
- ( - - ) to Leaders with a greater or equal number of Trade Routes compared to Xerxes
- ( + ) to Leaders with fewer Trade Routes than Xerxes

## Starting Bias
- Desert

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Assyria]], [[Maurya]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Mongolia]]
- Modern: [[Mughal]], [[Qajar]], [[Russia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Incense Censer***
	- +2 Culture per Age for every active Trade Route
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Gold Fluted Phiale***
	- +1 Trade Route Limit with all other Leaders
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Chalcedony Seal***
	- +1 Culture and Gold on Unique Buildings and Improvements
- Lvl 10 Title: **Shāh of the Silk Road**

> *Under my hand, wherever there is conflict and chaos I strike it down. I will have peace, even amidst ashes.*
