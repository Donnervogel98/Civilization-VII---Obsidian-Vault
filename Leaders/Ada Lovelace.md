>*With logic and soul, Ada Lovelace, you seek to understand the workings of the world. In the interplay of reason and calculation lies spirit. Set it free, bring your analytical engines to bear upon a world that needs reason, and see what new horizons you can imagine.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Enchantress of Number*
- Cities receive +2 Science per Age when you complete a Civic Mastery; this resets at the start of each Age
- Gain Culture equal to 100% of your total Science per turn when you complete a Technology Mastery

## Agenda
##### *Analytical Engine*
- ( + ) to the Leader with the most Masteries
- ( - ) to the Leader with the fewest Masteries

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Norman]]
- Modern: [[Great Britain]], [[Russia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Punch Cards***
	- Gain 20 Science Per Age for every Science Building Constructed
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Piano***
	- +10% Culture towards studying Civic Masteries
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Note G***
	- Every time you research a Technology or Civic Mastery, the Capital gains Production equal to 15% of its cost
- Lvl 10 Title: **Enchantress of Numbers**
