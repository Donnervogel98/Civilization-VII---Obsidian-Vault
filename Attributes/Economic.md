#### Traditions
Economic Time-Tested Civilizations gain access to the below Traditions through their unique Civic Trees
- Antiquity: **Merchant Class**
	- +1 Resource Capacity in Settlements with a Gold Building
- Exploration: **Supply and Demand**
	- +3 Gold for every Empire Resource you have
- Modern: **Gold Standard**
	- Double the base yield of Gold Buildings

#### Diplomacy
Economic Leaders gain access to the below diplomatic actions
- Endeavor: **Open Markets**
	- Accepted
		- You gain +4 Gold per Age per turn, and the receiver gains +2 Gold per Age per turn
	- Supported
		- Both Leaders gain +6 Gold per Age per turn
- Sanction: **Hinder Finances**
	- Target loses 10% of their Gold per turn

#### Legend Path Unlocks
Economic Leaders gain access to the below upon reaching the appropriate level in their [[Legend Paths|Legend Path]]
- **Attribute Node (Lvl 3 or Lvl 7)**
	- +25% Gold from Trade and Treasure Fleets
- **Dedication (Lvl 4 & Lvl 8)**
	- +15% Gold, -10% Food
