## Unique Ability
##### *Treasures of Nineveh*
- Gain a Technology and a Great Work upon capturing a Settlement for the first time
- [Ant/Exp] Great Works are not awarded from Technologies
- [Mod] Explorers cannot gain the ability to use the Excavate Artifacts action on Natural Wonders

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Quarter: *Ekallu*
- +2 Production for each Codex on display in this Settlement
- Building: **Citadel**
	- +3 Production
	- +2 Happiness if placed on Flat Terrain
	- +1 Production Adjacency for Wonders
	- Counts as a Fortification
- Building: **Royal Library**
	- +3 Science
	- Has 2 Codex Slots
	- +1 Happiness Adjacency for Rivers
	- +1 Science Adjacency for Wonders

## Unique Units
##### Cavalry Unit: *Magarru*
- +5 Combat Strength defending against Ranged Units
- +1 Movement
- Can move after attacking
##### Army Commander: *Turtanu*
- Infantry and Cavalry Units within the Command Radius have +5 Combat Strength against Fortified Districts

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Birtūtu*
- Building: **Citadel**
- Wonder: **Dur-Sharrukin**
- +1 Settlement Limit
- Mastery
	- Tradition: **Dūrāni I**
		- +2 Culture in the Capital for every Technology Mastery completed this Age
	- Gain 1 Codex
##### *Tupšarrūtu*
- Building: **Royal Library**
- Unlocks Army Commanders
- Mastery
	- Tradition: **Miḫiṣtu I**
		- +25% Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders with a Great Work Slot
	- Gain 1 Codex
##### *Kibrāt Arba’i*
- Tradition: **Girru I**
	- +5 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units within an Army Commander's Radius
	- +3 Production, Science, and Food in Captured Settlements from any Age
- +1 Settlement Limit
- Mastery
	- +1 Tradition slot
	- Gain 1 Codex

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Renaissance*
- +1 Settlement Limit
- Tradition: **Miḫiṣtu II**
	- +25% Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders with a Great Work Slot
	- +2 Science on displayed Great Works
- +1 Tradition slot
- Mastery
	- Tradition: **Girru II**
		- +5 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units within an Army Commander's Radius
		- +5 Production, Science, and Food in Captured Settlements from any Age
	- Gain 1 Relic
##### *Hierarchy*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Militaristic|Professional Army]] and [[Scientific|Alchemy]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Ashur I**
	- +5 Combat Strength for Infantry and Cavalry Units when attacking Fortified Districts

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Dūrāni II**
	- +2 Culture in the Capital and in Settlements not founded by you that were conquered in this Age for every Technology Mastery completed this Age
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Militaristic|Force Structuring]] and [[Scientific|Location Theory]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Ashur I**
	- +1 Science on Fortification Buildings
	- +5 Combat Strength for Infantry and Cavalry Units when attacking Fortified Districts

## Associated Wonder
##### *Dur-Sharrukin*
- Unlocked for any Civilization by the *Discipline* Civic
- +1 Science on Fortifications in this Settlement
- +5 Combat Strength for Fortified Districts in all Settlements
- Acts as a Fortified District that must be conquered
- Must be placed adjacent to a District

## Starting Biases
- Flat
- Rivers
