Bend your mind towards the artistry of war, Friedrich – your orchestra awaits your signal.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Berlin Academy*
- Army Commanders start with the Merit Commendation, granting them +1 Command Radius
- Gain an Infantry Unit when you complete a Technology Mastery or construct a Science Building
- +2 Science per Age for each Commendation earned by your Commanders

## Agenda
##### *To Arms!*
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the fewest Military Units on the map
- ( + ) to the Leader with the most Military Units on the map

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Egypt]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Walking Stick***
	- +1 Science per Age on Military Buildings
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Order of the Black Eagle Badge***
	- Gain 50 Production when you spend an Attribute point on the Militaristic Attribute tree
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Krone von Friedrich I***
	- +2 Combat Strength for All Units when using Coordinated Attack or Focus Fire
- Lvl 10 Title: **Old Fritz**
