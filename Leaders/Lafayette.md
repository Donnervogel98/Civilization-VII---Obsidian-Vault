[[Cultural]], [[Diplomatic]]

>*Gilbert du Motier, the Marquis de Lafayette, the Hero of Two Worlds, you stand on the knife's edge. On one side is heartless autocracy, and on the other mindless anarchy, with only reason and compassion leading your forward. Tread carefully, Marquis, one missed step risks a fall.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Hero of Two Worlds*
- Gains a unique Endeavor called **Reform**, which grants an additional Tradition slot; supporting this Endeavor also grants the other Leader an additional Tradition slot, otherwise they are granted a Social Policy slot
- +1 Combat Strength for every Tradition, but not Social Policy, slotted in the Government
- +1 Culture and Happiness per Age in Settlements, doubled in Distant Lands

## Agenda
##### *French Quarters*
- ( + ) to the Leader with the most Urban Districts that have all Building slots filled
- ( - ) to the Leader with the least Urban Districts

## Starting Bias
- Coast

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[America]], [[French Empire]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Letter to Adrienne***
	- +2 Happiness per Age for each Social Policy slotted in your Government
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Hermione Model***
	- +1 Movement to all Naval Units
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Tricolor Cockade***
	- +2 Culture and Happiness per Age in the Capital for every Tradition, but not Policy, slotted into your Government
- Lvl 10 Title: **Hero of Two Worlds**
