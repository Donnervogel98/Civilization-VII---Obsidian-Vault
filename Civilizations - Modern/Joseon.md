## Unique Ability
##### *Munchi*
- Increased Specialist Limit in the Capital
- Decreased Specialist Limit in all Cities other than the Capital
- [Mod] Cities other than the Capital with a Seowon instead have an increased Specialist Limit
- Joseon begins the Age with a set City Limit
- Can purchase Tier 1 Science and Culture Buildings in Towns

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Quarter: *Seowon*
- This Settlement receives a percentage of its Production as Culture
- If constructed in a Town, the Capital receives increased Population
- Building: **Confucian Academy**
	- Increased Culture
	- Increased Happiness Adjacency with Mountains and Wonders
	- Can be purchased in Towns, but cannot be built in the Capital
- Building: **Printing House**
	- Increased Science
	- Increased Production Adjacency with Quarters and Wonders
	- Can be purchased in Towns, but cannot be built in the Capital

## Unique Units
##### Army Commander: *Jangyongyoung*
- Land Units in a Capital receive increased Combat Strength within the Command Radius
##### Heavy Naval Unit: *Turtle Ship*
- Decreased Ranged and Bombard Strength compared to other Heavy Naval Units
- Has increased Bombard Strength and increased Ranged Strength when on Coast Terrain
