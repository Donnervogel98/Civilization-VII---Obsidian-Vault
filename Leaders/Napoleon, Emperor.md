## Leader Bonus
##### *Continental System*
- All basic Sanctions are automatically unlocked
- +50% Influence towards initiating Sanctions
- +5 Culture and Gold per Age for each Leader you have an active Sanction on, except Denouncement
- +1 Combat Strength for land Units for every Sanction you have active, except Denouncement

## Agenda
##### *Napoleonic Code*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the smallest standing army
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the largest standing army

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Portrait of Josephine***
	- +2 Gold per Age for every Leader you are Unfriendly or Hostile with
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Green Colonel's Jacket***
	- +1 Gold per Age per Trade Route, doubled if you have the highest number of Trade Routes
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Crown of Napoleon***
	- Gain 200 Gold per Age when a Formal War is declared against you
- Lvl 10 Title: **Self-Crowned Emperor**
