>*Maurya life thrums to the thud of ox hoofs returning from the fields, the clatter of palm-lead texts, the beat of the bhambha drums, the steady rhythm of monks' chants, the whisper of court attendants. Lend your voice, lead the song.*

## Unique Ability
##### *Dhamma Lipi*
- +2/+2/+3 Happiness on Happiness, Military, and Science Buildings
- Land Units are fully healed when you enter a Celebration

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Quarter: *Matha*
- +10% Happiness in this Settlement
- Building: **Dharamshala**
	- +3 Happiness
	- +1 Science Adjacency for Quarters and Wonders
- Building: **Vihara**
	- +3 Happiness
	- +1 Culture Adjacency for Mountains and Wonders

## Unique Units
##### Cavalry Unit: *Purabhettarah*
- +5 Combat Strength against Fortified Districts
##### Settler: *Nagarika*
- Grants a free purchase of an Altar Building in the new Town

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Acharya*
- Building: **Dharmashala**
- Building: **Vihara**
- Tradition: **Charvaka I**
	- +1 Gold for every 5 excess Happiness in Cities
- Mastery
	- Unlocks founding a Pantheon, or grants an additional belief if you have already founded one
##### *Vyuham*
- Tradition: **Kshatriya** 
	- +3 Combat Strength for Infantry and Cavalry Units when adjacent to each other, or +5 while in a Celebration
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Mantriparishad*
- Tradition: **Arthashastra I**
	- +1 Science for every 5 excess Happiness in Cities
- Wonder: **Sanchi Stupa**
- +1 Tradition slot

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Renaissance*
- Tradition: **Charvaka II**
	- +1 Gold for every 5 excess Happiness in Cities
	- +10% Production in Cities while in a Celebration
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Hierarchy*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Militaristic|Professional Army]] and [[Scientific|Alchemy]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Chhatrapati I**
	- +2 Production and Science on the Palace and on City Halls in Captured Settlements from any Age, doubled while in a Celebration

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Arthashastra I**
	- +1 Science for every 5 excess Happiness in Cities
	- +10% Science while in a Celebration
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Militaristic|Force Structuring]] and [[Scientific|Location Theory]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Chhatrapati II**
	- +3 Production and Science on the Palace and on City Halls in Captured Settlements from any Age, doubled while in a Celebration

## Associated Wonder
##### *Sanchi Stupa*
- Unlocked for any Civilization by the *Skilled Trades* Civic
- +3 Happiness
- +1 Culture for every 5 excess Happiness in this Settlement
- Must be placed on Plains
