[[Cultural]], [[Scientific]]

>*Catherine the Great, Tsarina of the Romanovs, you bring beauty to autocracy. With your keen eye, you transform not only your palace, but your people. Cultivate a love of the arts – and respect for authority. Guide them to knowledge, and let them see the light of the north star, shining out from your throne.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Star of the North*
- +2 Culture per Age on displayed Great Works
- Buildings and Wonders with Great Work slots gain 1 additional slot
- Cities settled in Tundra gain Science equal to 25% of their Culture per turn

## Agenda
##### *Dusha*
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the most Great Works
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the fewest Great Works
- Only triggers if Catherine the Great has at least one Great Work

## Starting Bias
- Tundra

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Bulgaria]], [[Mongolia]], [[Norman]]
- Modern: [[Prussia]], [[Russia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Eagle Banner***
	- Cities gain 2% Science per Great Work on display (Max 20%)
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Potemkin's Sword-Knot***
	- +1 Movement for Commanders
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Great Imperial Crown***
	- +2 Science per Age in Towns in Tundra
- Lvl 10 Title: **Star of the North**
