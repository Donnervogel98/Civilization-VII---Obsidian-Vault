## Leader Bonus
##### *Pambansang Bayani*
- When gaining rewards from a Narrative Event, gain an additional 20 Culture, Gold, and Influence per Age
- Has additional Narrative Events
- +50% Celebration duration
- +50% Happiness towards Celebrations

## Agenda
##### *Kapwa*
- ( + + ) for the Leader with the most active Endeavors started, or ( + ) to each if there is a tie
- ( - - ) for the Leader with the most active Sanctions started, or ( - ) to each if there is a tie

## Starting Biases
- Tropical

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Maya]], [[Mississippian]], [[Tonga]]
- Exploration: [[Hawai'i]], [[Iceland]], [[Majapahit]]
- Modern: [[Meiji Japan]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Forget-me-not***
	- Gain 10 Happiness per Age for completing Narrative Events
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Ophthalmoscope***
	- +25% Celebration Duration
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Noli Me Tángere***
	- +10% to Science, Culture, and Gold for each Legacy Path completed in this Age
- Lvl 10 Title: **National Hero**
