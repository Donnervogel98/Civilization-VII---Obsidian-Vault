In their walled cities, the rulers of Aksum carve their legacy in stone and raise it to the heavens. Take the rudder and hoist the sails of Aksum, and steer your crew to luxury or conquest. Raise the hawelti and herald Aksum as the lords of the age.*

## Unique Ability
##### *Kingdom of Natural Wealth*
- +3/+3/+4 Gold on Resources in Settlements adjacent to Coast
- +1 Trade Route Limit with other Civilizations, and all Civilizations have +1 Trade Route Limit with you
- Trade Routes to or from your Settlements have unlimited Range

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Improvement: *Hawilt*
- +2 Gold
- +1 Culture for adjacent Wonders and other Hawilti
- Must be placed on Flat Terrain

## Unique Units
##### Heavy Naval Unit: *Dhow*
- +4 Combat Strength on Coast
- Can cross borders of other Civilizations, and has 1 charge to create a Trade Route
##### Trade Ship: *Tankwa*
- Cannot be pillaged
- +2 Sight

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Periplus of the Erythraean Sea*
- Tradition: **Port of Nations I**
	- +2 Culture and Gold for every active Trade Route
	- +100% Trade Income in Settlements adjacent to Coast
- Wonder: **Great Stele**
##### *Monumentum Adulitanum*
- Tradition: **Throne Of My Fathers**
	- +15% Gold in Cities adjacent to Coast
	- Gain Culture equal to 25% of your Trade Income
- Improvement: **Hawilt**
##### *Book of the Himyarites*
- Tradition **May This Please The People I**
	- +2 Culture on Resources on or adjacent to Coast
- +1 Tradition slot

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Renaissance*
- Tradition: **May This Please The People II**
	- +3 Culture on Resources on or adjacent to Coast
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Hierarchy*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Cultural|Classical Revival]] and [[Economic|Supply and Demand]]
- Wonder: **Notre Dame**
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Incense Road I**
	- +1 Production in the Capital for every active Trade Route
	- Trade Routes gain +2 Sight and cannot be pillaged

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Port of Nations II**
	- +3 Culture and Gold for every active Trade Route
	- +200% Trade Income in Settlements adjacent to Coast
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Cultural|Romanticism]] and [[Economic|Gold Standard]]
- Wonder: **Taj Mahal**
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Incense Road I**
	- +2 Production in the Capital for every active Trade Route
	- Trade Routes gain +2 Sight and cannot be pillaged

## Associated Wonder
##### *Great Stele*
- +2 Production
- Gain 200 Gold per Age when you construct a Wonder, including this one
- Must be placed on Flat Terrain
