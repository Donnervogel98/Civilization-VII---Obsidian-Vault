>*Sing, Muse, of the King of the World, the Hero of Hellas, Son of Zeus and Ammon. From the hills of Macedonia to the edges of Oceanus, your name will mark the bounds of civilization. Take the reins, Alexander. Lead the charge and fulfill your fate.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *King of the World*
- Cities other than your Capital with a Wonder get +10% Production and Culture, doubled if they are on a different Continent than your Capital
- +2 Combat Strength for Land Military Units; +1 Combat Strength for Unique Military Units for every City with a Wonder other than your Capital (max 6)
- +2 Dominion for every City with a Wonder other than your Capital
- Towns converted into Cities are renamed after Alexander

## Agenda
##### *Godlike Basileus*
- ( + ) to Leaders who have the fewest conquered Settlements
- ( - - ) to Leaders who have the most conquered Settlements.

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Greece]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Mongolia]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Ottomans]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***The Iliad***
	- +1 Culture and Production per Age on Wonders in Cities other than your Capital
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Gordian Knot***
	- +1 Settlement Limit in each Age; +1 Gold Maintenance per Age on all Land Units
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Bridle of Bucephalus***
	- +3 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units, but +25% Production and Purchase cost for Cavalry Units
- Lvl 10 Title: **The Great**
