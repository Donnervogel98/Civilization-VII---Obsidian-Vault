*Alternate Persona of [[Himiko, Queen of Wa]]*
*Available with the [[Founders Content Pack]] DLC*

![Portrait](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/1/15/Himiko%2C_High_Shaman_%28Civ7%29.png/revision/latest//scale-to-width-down/350)

[[Cultural]], [[Diplomatic]]

>*Himiko, shaman-queen of Yamatai, stands between worlds. As you bring the power of the land, you turn forests to shrines, waterfalls to music, and the mountains themselves to allies. The divine wind seeks to blow; great queen, direct its path.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Miko of Amaterasu*
- +2 Happiness per Age on Happiness and Diplomacy Buildings, doubled if a Building is both
- +50% Production towards constructing Happiness and Diplomacy Buildings, doubled if a Building is both
- +15% Culture but -15% Science, and these effects are doubled while in a Celebration

## Agenda
##### *Shaman Queen*
- ( - ) to a Leader for each of their Settlements with constructed Science and Gold Buildings
- ( + ) to a Leader for each of their Settlements with constructed Culture and Happiness Buildings

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Khmer]], [[Mississippian]], [[Tonga]]
- Exploration: [[Hawai'i]], [[Majapahit]]
- Modern: [[Meiji Japan]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Yata no Kagami***
	- Gain 20 Culture per Age at the start of every Celebration
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Sakaki Branch***
	- +25% Production towards constructing Happiness Buildings
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Kusanagi no Tsurugi***
	- +3 Culture per Age on Happiness Buildings but -1 Science per Age on Happiness Buildings
- Lvl 10 Title: **Avatar of Amaterasu**

>*The sun shines upon me, her daughter. Upon my enemies, it will burn.*
