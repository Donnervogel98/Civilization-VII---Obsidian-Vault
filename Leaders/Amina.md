>*Amina, sarauniya of the Hausa, bowing to none, prepare your mount for ware. You who would not serve, protected those who served you behind high walls – and rode down those who would not. Fight now, sarauniya, and gain what is yours.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Warrior-Queen of Zazzau*
- +1 Resource Capacity in Cities
- +1 Gold per Age for each Resource assigned to Cities
- +5 Combat Strength on all Units in Plains and Desert

## Agenda
##### *Desert of the Warrior Queen*
- ( - - ) to Leaders with more Settlements on Plains or Desert Terrain than Amina
- ( + ) to Leaders with no Settlements on Plains or Desert Terrain

## Starting Bias
- Desert
- Plains

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Aksum]], [[Egypt]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Republic of Pirates]], [[Songhai]]
- Modern: [[Buganda]], [[Mexico]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Takoba***
	- +1 Combat Strength for all Units in Plains and Desert
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Kwalkwali***
	- +1 Gold per Age for each Resource assigned to Cities
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Rawani***
	- +3 Combat Strength to a Unit's next attack after they destroy an enemy Unit
- Lvl 10 Title: **Warrior-Queen**
