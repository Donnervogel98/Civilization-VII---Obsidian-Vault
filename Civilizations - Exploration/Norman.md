[[Diplomatic]], [[Militaristic]]

>*Restless conquerors, the Normans seek out new lands, and, with their mighty stone castles, they hold what they take. Norman society is made of courtly knights and chivalry, chevalers and keeps. It offers protection—under the sword. Stand and receive the fealty of your knights – the Norman yoke is upon the land.*

## Unique Ability
##### *Normannitas*
- +5 Combat Strength for Land Units and Fortified Districts adjacent to Coast
- +25 HP on Fortified Districts

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Quarter: *Donjon*
- +10% Production towards training Cavalry Units
- Receive a free Chevaler Unit when completed
- Building: **Bailey**
	- +6 Culture
	- +1 Culture Adjacency for Fortification Constructibles and Wonders
	- Counts as a Fortification
- Building: **Motte**
	- +6 Happiness
	- +4 Happiness if placed on Rough Terrain
	- +1 Happiness Adjacency for Wonders
	- Counts as a Fortification

## Unique Units
##### Cavalry Unit: *Chevaler*
- +3 Combat Strength against slower Units
##### Settler: *Sokeman*
- Receive Medieval Walls in the City Center when a new Town is created

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Origins*
- Tradition: **Juré I**
	- +1 Culture in the Capital and +1 Happiness in Cities for every Tradition slotted in the Government
- +1 Settlement Limit
- Mastery
	- Tradition: **Palisading I**
		- +50% Production towards constructing Fortification Constructibles
	- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Foundation*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Diplomatic|Priestly Class]] and [[Militaristic|Warrior Class]] 
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Sauveté I**
	- +1 Culture and Gold on Fortified Districts, doubled in Captured Settlements from any Age
	- +25 HP on Fortified Districts

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Consuetudines et Justicie*
- Building: **Motte**
- Tradition: **Palisading I**
	- +50% Production towards constructing Fortification Constructibles
	- +1 Happiness and Production on Fortified Districts
- Mastery
	- Building: **Bailey**
	- Tradition: **Familia Regis**
		- The Palace gains a +1 Happiness Adjacency for Fortification Constructibles
	- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Common Law*
- Tradition: **Juré II**
	- +2 Culture in the Capital and +2 Happiness in Cities for every Tradition slotted in the Government
- +1 Tradition slot
- Mastery
	- Tradition: **Servitium Debitum**
		- +1 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units for every Tradition slotted in the Government
		- -2 Gold Maintenance for Cavalry Units
	- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Domesday Book*
- Tradition: **Bayeux Tapestry I**
	- +4 Culture in Captured Settlements from any Age
	- Gain a free Fortification purchase in Captured Settlements
- Wonder: **White Tower**
- +1 Settlement Limit

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Bayeux Tapestry I**
	- +6 Culture in Captured Settlements from any Age
	- Gain a free Fortification purchase in Captured Settlements
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Diplomatic|Vaudeville]] and [[Militaristic|Force Structuring]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Sauveté II**
	- +2 Culture and Gold on Fortified Districts, doubled in Captured Settlements from any Age
	- +25 HP on Fortified Districts

## Associated Wonder
##### *White Tower*
- Unlocked for any Civilization by the *Sovereignty II* Civic
- +4 Happiness
- +4 Happiness in this Settlement for every Tradition slotted in the Government
- Must be placed adjacent to a City Hall
