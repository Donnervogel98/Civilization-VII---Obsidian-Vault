#### Civics
Diplomatic Time-Tested Civilizations gain access to one of the below sets of Traditions through their unique Civic Trees
- ***Influence***
	- Antiquity: **Emissaries**
		- +25% Influence towards initiating Endeavors
		- +1 Gold from active Endeavors you started or supported
	- Exploration: **Spy Network**
		- +30% Influence towards initiating Espionage actions
		- +2 Gold from active Espionage actions you started
	- Modern: **The Great Game**
		- +40% Influence towards initiating Sanctions
		- +3 Gold from active Sanctions you started
- ***Happiness***
	- Antiquity: **Priestly Class**
		- Happiness Buildings cost no Maintenance in Cities
	- Exploration: **Jubilee**
		- +3 to all Yields in your Capital during a Celebration
	- Modern: **Vaudeville**
		- Double the base yield of Happiness Buildings
#### Diplomacy
Diplomatic Leaders gain access to the below diplomatic actions
- Endeavor: **Local Festival**
	- Accepted
		- You gain +2 Happiness per Age in Cities, and the receiver gains +2 Influence per Age
	- Supported
		- Both Leaders gain +3 Happiness per Age in Cities
- Sanction: **Hinder Public Morale**
	- Target loses 10% of their Happiness per turn

#### Legend Path Unlocks
Diplomatic Leaders gain access to the below upon reaching the appropriate level in their [[Legend Paths|Legend Path]]
- **Attribute Node (Lvl 3 or Lvl 7)**
	- +25% Relationship from starting or supporting Endeavors. -25% Relationship from starting Sanctions or rejecting Endeavors
- **Dedication (Lvl 4 & Lvl 8)**
	- +15% Influence, -2 Combat Strength for Naval (Exp.) or Air (Mod.) Units
