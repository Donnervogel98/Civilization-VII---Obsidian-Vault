## Leader Bonus
##### *El Libertador*
- Gain 2 War Support on Wars you declare
- When you conquer a Settlement for the first time the Settlement can purchase 1 Building or Improvement for free
- Unrest does not prevent Purchasing

## Agenda
##### *Cornerstone of Freedom*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the fewest Unhappy Settlements
- ( - ) to the Leader with the most Unhappy Settlements

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Maya]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Inca]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Mexico]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Gold Snuff Box***
	- +20 Food per Age in Cities when in only one Alliance
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Davalos Medal***
	- +1 Happiness per Age on Military Buildings
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Letter to Jamaica***
	- +50% Gold towards purchasing Units during a Celebration
- Lvl 10 Title: **The Liberator**
