[[Expansionist]], [[Scientific]]

>*Master Kong, you gave guidance to rulers when they were lost. Now, wise one, the world is again in need of your guidance. Help us to temper duty with benevolence; help us to see with wisdom and propriety. Where the darkness of ignorance falls, lead us in the righteous path.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Keju*
- +25% Growth Rate in Cities
- +2 Science from Specialists

## Agenda
##### *Guanxi*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the most Specialists
- ( - ) to the Leader with the fewest Specialists
- Only triggers if Confucius has at least one Specialist

## Starting Bias
- Grassland

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Han]]
- Exploration: [[Ming]], [[Mongolia]]
- Modern: [[Qing]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Brush & Scroll***
	- +5% Growth Rate in Cities for every Specialist in that City (Max 25%)
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Altar Set***
	- +1 Culture from Specialists
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***The Analects***
	- +1 Science from Specialists
- Lvl 10 Title: **Great Sage**
