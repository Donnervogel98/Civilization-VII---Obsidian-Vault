## Unlocked
- Plunder two Trade Routes
- Civilizations
	- [[Aksum]]
	- [[Carthage]]
- Leaders
	- [[Amina]]
	- [[Edward Teach]]
	- [[Sayyida al Hurra]]

## Unique Ability
##### *Flying Gang*
- Naval Units, Treasure Convoys, and Fleet Commanders can move into other Civilizations' borders without being at War or having Open Borders
- [Exp/Mod] Fleet Commanders gain 1 charge to create a new Settlement after receiving their first Promotion
- Settlers cannot be trained or purchased, but you can capture other Civilizations' Settlers
- [Ant] Gain a free Settler upon completing the Sailing and Navigation Technologies and the Pirate Origins Civic
- +5 GDP from your Naval Units defeating other Units

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Quarter: *Haven*
- +1 Gold for every Treasure Resource in your Pool
- Friendly Naval Units receive +50 Healing in this tile
- Building: **Naval Arsenal**
	- +4 Gold
	- +1 Gold Adjacency for Resources and Wonders
	- Must be placed on Coast
- Building: **Naval Station**
	- +5 Production
	- +1 Production Adjacency for Military Buildings, Gold Buildings, and Wonders
	- Must be placed on Coast
	- Has no Gold Maintenance

## Unique Units
##### Light Naval Unit: *Sloop*
- Has the Pirate ability
- Defeating a Unit with the Sloop provides Gold equal to 100% of that Unit's Combat Strength
- Can move after attacking and has 5 Movement
##### Fleet Commander: *Buccaneer*
- Gains 1 charge to use the **Raiding Party** Action every 2 Levels it has
	- Targets an adjacent Treasure Resource owned by a different Civilization in Distant Lands
	- This Settlement instantly spawns a Treasure Convoy under your control
	- This does not reset the turns until another Treasure Convoy spawns

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Origins*
- Tradition: **Black Flag I**
	- +50 Gold (Scales with Game Speed) from Plundering Trade Routes
	- +50% Yields and Healing from Coastal Raiding with Naval Units
- Gain 1 Settler for Free
- +1 Tradition slot
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Foundation*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Economic|Merchant Class]] and [[Militaristic|Warrior Class]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Brethren of the Coast I**
	- +2 Production on Districts on or adjacent to Coast
	- -1 Happiness on Districts not on or adjacent to Coast

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Articles of Agreement*
- Building: **Naval Station**
- Tradition: **Press Gangs I**
	- +100% Production and Gold towards training Light Naval Units
	- Fleet Commanders gain the Looting Promotion for free
##### *Ports of Call*
- Building: **Naval Arsenal**
- Tradition: **Merry Life and a Short One**
	- When you defeat a Support Unit, gain Gold equal to the Unit’s Production cost
	- When you defeat a Naval Unit, gain Gold equal to 25% of the Unit’s Production cost
- Mastery
	- Wonder: **Havana Harbor**
	- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Enemy of All Nations*
- Tradition: **Black Flag I**
	- +100 Gold (Scales with Game Speed) from Plundering Trade Routes
	- +100% Yields and Healing from Coastal Raiding with Naval Units
	- Fleet Commanders and Naval Units gain Stealth on Atolls
- +15 Combat Strength for all Military Units against Treasure Convoys
- Mastery
	- +2 Cargo from captured Treasure Convoys

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Press Gangs II**
	- +150% Production and Gold towards training Light Naval Units
	- Fleet Commanders gain the Looting Promotion for free
- +1 Tradition slot
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Economic|Gold Standard]] and [[Militaristic|Force Structuring]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Brethren of the Coast II**
	- +3 Production on Districts on or adjacent to Coast
	- -2 Happiness on Districts not on or adjacent to Coast

## Associated Wonder
##### *Havana Harbor*
- Unlocked for any Civilization by the *Shipbuilding II* Technology
- +3 Gold
- This Settlement generates Treasure Convoys worth 3 Cargo in the Exploration Age
- +1 Economic Attribute Point
- Must be built in Distant Lands Coast adjacent to Land that is not a Lake
