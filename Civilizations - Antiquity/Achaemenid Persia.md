[[Economic]], [[Militaristic]]

>*Persia sits astride the world, a kingdom so great that its residents call it simply Xšāça, “the Kingdom.” Commanding trade routes and the armies to defend them, the rulers of Persia claim all that is worth having for their own.*

## Unique Ability
##### *Hamarana Council*
- +3 Combat Strength for Infantry Units when attacking
- Army Commanders start with a free Promotion

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Improvement: *Pairidaeza*
- +2 Culture and +1 Gold
- +5 Healing for Military Units in a Settlement with a Pairidaeza
- Cannot be built adjacent to another Pairidaeza

## Unique Units
##### Infantry Unit: *Immortal*
- Heals 15 HP after defeating an enemy Unit
##### Army Commander: *Hazarapatis*
- Starts with the Initiative Promotion

## Civics – Antiquity
##### *Spada*
- Tradition: **Kara I**
	- +25% Production towards training Infantry Units
	- -2 Gold Maintenance for Infantry Units
- Wonder: **Gate of All Nations**
##### *Satrapies*
- Improvement: **Pairidaeza**
- Tradition: **Čāpār-Kāna I**
	- +3 Gold in Towns, doubled in Captured Towns from any Age
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Baziš*
- Tradition: **Shahanshah I**
	- +3 Combat Strength for Units in enemy territory
	- +5 Culture and Gold for every different Civilization you have captured a Settlement from
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot

## Civics – Exploration
##### *Renaissance*
- Tradition: **Kara II**
	- +25% Production towards training Infantry Units
	- -3 Gold Maintenance for Infantry Units
	- Infantry Units heal 15 HP after defeating an enemy Unit
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Hierarchy*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Economic|Supply and Demand]] and [[Militaristic|Professional Army]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Fractal Rule I**
	- +1 Culture on Unique Improvements
	- +1 Gold on Unique Improvements and Buildings in Captured Settlements from any Age

## Civics – Modern
##### *Modernization*
- Tradition: **Čāpār-Kāna II**
	- +6 Gold in Towns, doubled in Captured Towns from any Age
- Tradition: **Shahanshah II**
	- +3 Combat Strength for Units fighting in enemy territory
	- +10 Culture and Gold for every different Civilization you have captured a Settlement from
- +1 Settlement Limit
- +1 Tradition slot
##### *Administration*
- Attribute Traditions: [[Economic|Gold Standard]] and [[Militaristic|Force Structuring]]
- +1 Settlement Limit
##### *Syncretism*
- Affirmation Tradition: **Fractal Rule II**
	- +2 Culture on Unique Improvements
	- +2 Gold on Unique Improvements and Buildings in Captured Settlements from any Age

## Associated Wonder
##### *Gate of All Nations*
- Unlocked for any Civilization by the *Discipline II* Civic
- +2 Happiness
- +1 War Support on all wars
- Must be placed adjacent to a District

## Starting Biases
- Desert
