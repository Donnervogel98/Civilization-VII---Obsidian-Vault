*Alternate Persona of [[Napoleon, Emperor]]*
*Available with a linked 2K account that is also linked to Civilization VI*

  ![Napoleon, Revolutionary](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/4/4b/Napoleon%2C_Revolutionary_%28Civ7%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/350)

[[Cultural]], [[Militaristic]]

>*Napoleon Bonaparte, your name is synonymous with ambition – and audacity. In the chaos of revolution, you bring order – your order, arranged and enacted by your hand. Bring your keen strategic senses and unstoppable drive to overturn the world and ensure that, when the smoke clears, you remain.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Enemy of the Coalitions*
- +1 Movement for all Land Units
- Defeating an enemy Unit provides Culture equal to 100% of its Combat Strength
- The first time each Age that a war is declared against you, gain an Army Commander in your Capital packed with 1 Infantry Unit, 1 Cavalry Unit, 1 Ranged Unit, and 1 Siege Unit, even if those Units have not been unlocked

## Agenda
##### *Culture from Conquest*
- ( - ) for every Alliance made between other Leaders
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the highest Culture per turn

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Artilleryman's Glones***
	- +2 Culture for each Army Commander Promotion
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Bicorne Hat***
	- +1 Movement for Infantry Units
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Legion d'honneur Grand Eagle and Cross***
	- Units in a Command Radius heal +10 HP after defeating an enemy Unit
- Lvl 10 Title: **Man of Destiny**
