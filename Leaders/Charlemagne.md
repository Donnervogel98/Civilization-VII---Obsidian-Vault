>*The clarion call of the knight's trumpet seeks you out, Charlemagne, King of the Franks and Holy Roman Emperor. In feast-halls and in troubadours' songs, may your victories resound. In legend, may your victories remain. As your knights take the battlefield, let them know for whom they ride.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Father of Europe*
- Military and Science Buildings receive a +1 Happiness adjacency for Fortifications
- Gain 2 free Cavalry Units, once unlocked, when entering a Celebration
- +5 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units during a Celebration

## Agenda
##### *The Golden Shepherd*
- ( + + ) to the Leader who has triggered the most Celebrations, or ( + ) to each if there is a tie
- ( - - ) to the Leader who has triggered the fewest Celebrations, or ( - ) to each if there is a tie

## Starting Biases
- Rivers

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Greece]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Bulgaria]], [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Joyeuse***
	- +1 Happiness per Age on Cavalry Units Stationed on a Palace or City Hall
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Globus Cruciger***
	- +50 Influence when you spend an Attribute Point on the Diplomatic Attribute tree
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Tencendur***
	- +1 Movement for Cavalry Units
- Lvl 10 Title: **Father of Europe**
