*Alternate Persona of [[Friedrich, Oblique]]*
*Available with the [[Deluxe Content Pack]] DLC*

  ![Friedrich, Baroque](http://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/6/60/Friedrich%2C_Baroque_%28Civ7%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/350)

[[Cultural]], [[Militaristic]]

>*Though a lover of the opera house more than the battlefield, it is in the cannon's fire that you shine. The line of attack replaces the violin's bow, the roll of cannon stands in for the timpani, and the brass horn now calls forth iron shot. Bend your mind towards the artistry of war, Friedrich – your orchestra awaits your signal.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Hohenfriedberger Marsch*
- Gain a Great Work when you capture a Settlement for the first time
- +1 Culture per Age on displayed Great Works
- Gain an Infantry Unit when you complete a Civic Mastery or construct a Culture Building

## Agenda
##### *Parisian Sensibilities*
- ( + + ) to a Leader for each built Wonder in their Capital
- ( - ) to a Leader for each built Building in their Capital

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Egypt]], [[Rome]]
- Exploration: [[Norman]], [[Spain]]
- Modern: [[French Empire]], [[Prussia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Flute***
	- +1 Culture per Age on Military Buildings
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Voltaire's Edits***
	- Gain 50 Culture when you spend an Attribute Point on the Cultural Attribute tree
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Anti-Machiavel***
	- +2 free War Support on all wars declared against you
- Lvl 10 Title: **Without Worries**
