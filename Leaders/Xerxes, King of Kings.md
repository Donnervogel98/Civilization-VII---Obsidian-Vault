You conceive of a new world, one of wonders rising from the wilderness, of an empire that defines time, and one that will never forget your name – Xerxes.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Crusher of Rebellions*
- +3 Combat Strength for Units that are attacking in neutral or enemy territory
- Gain 100 Culture and Gold per Age when you capture a Settlement for the first time
- +10% Gold in all Settlements, doubled in Captured Settlements from any Age
- +1 Settlement limit each Age

## Agenda
##### *Lord of Fire*
- ( - - ) to Leaders who are not at war
- ( + ) to Leaders who are at war

## Starting Bias
- Desert

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Assyria]], [[Maurya]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Mongolia]]
- Modern: [[Mughal]], [[Qajar]], [[Russia]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Scythian Battle-Axe***
	- +1 Gold per Age for every Commander XP earned
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Lotus Blossom***
	- Gain Science and Culture per turn equal to 4% of your Gold income
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Golden Sceptre***
	- +3 Gold per age for each other Civilization's Settlement you have conquered
- Lvl 10 Title: **King of Kings**
