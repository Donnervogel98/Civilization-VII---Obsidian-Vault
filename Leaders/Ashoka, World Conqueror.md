*Alternate Persona of [[Ashoka, World Renouncer]]*
*Available with the [[Founders Content Pack]] DLC*

![Portrait](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/1/19/Ashoka%2C_World_Conqueror_%28Civ7%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/350)

[[Diplomatic]], [[Militaristic]]

>*Chakravartin Ashoka, the wheel-turning monarch, your success in war made plain to you its costs. Determined to end the cycle of violence that marked your time as emperor of Maurya, you brought wisdom instead of swords, the dharma in place of destruction, and a message of non-violence to all beings. The world now needs your wisdom.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Devaraja*
- +1 Production for every 5 excess Happiness in Cities
- +10% Production in Settlements not founded by you
- Declaring a Formal War grants a Celebration
- +5 Combat Strength against Fortified Districts for all Units during a Celebration

## Agenda
##### *Without Regret*
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the largest empire (in tiles)
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the smallest empire (in tiles)

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Khmer]], [[Maurya]]
- Exploration: [[Chola]], [[Hawai'i]]
- Modern: [[Mughal]], [[Nepal]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Chanda-mahasena***
	- +3 Combat Strength against Fortified Districts for all Units during a Celebration
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Silk Uttariya***
	- +2 Production per Age in Cities with positive Happiness
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Lion Capital***
	- Commanders receive +50% XP during a Celebration
- Lvl 10 Title: **World-Conqueror**
