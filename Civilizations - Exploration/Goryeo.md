## Unique Ability
##### *Byeongjo*
- Increased Culture from active Endeavors you started or supported
- Increased Culture on Unique Improvements in Towns and increased Influence on Unique Improvements in Cities
- [Mod] Increased Tourism from all Unique Improvements

## Unique Infrastructure
##### Improvement: *Gama*
- Increased Culture
- Increased Production for each adjacent Warehouse Building
- Must be placed on Vegetated Terrain

## Unique Units
##### Siege Unit: *Hwacha*
- Deals Splash damage equal to a percentage of the total damage done to the target
##### Cavalry Unit: *Tobang*
- Increased Combat Strength when Stationed or Occupying a District
- Increased Influence for each City Center with a Stationed Tobang
