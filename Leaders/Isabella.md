## Leader Bonus
##### *Seven Cities of Gold*
- Gain 100 Gold per Age every time you discover a Natural Wonder; this is doubled if the Natural Wonder is in Distant Lands
- +50% tile yields from Natural Wonders for every Natural Wonder in your Empire
- +50% Gold toward purchasing Naval Units
- -1 Gold Maintenance for Naval Units

## Agenda
##### *Wonderlust*
- ( - - - ) for each Natural Wonder within a Leader's borders
- ( + ) to Leaders with no Natural Wonders
