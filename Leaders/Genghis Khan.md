## Leader Bonus
##### *Chosen of Tengri*
- +3 Combat Strength for Cavalry Units, or +5 when in an Army Command Radius
- Army Commanders have +1 Movement even when no Units are packed into them
- Once per Age, Army Commanders can use the *Convert Independents* action to convert adjacent Independent Military Units to your control

## Agenda
##### *Tümen*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the fewest Cavalry Units
- ( - ) to the Leader with the most Cavalry Units

- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Paiza***
	- -1 Gold Maintenance on Cavalry Units
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Saddle***
	- +1 Food per Age on Cavalry Units Stationed on a Palace or City Hall
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Sulde***
	- +1 Culture per Age on Cavalry Units Stationed on a Palace or City Hall
- Lvl 10 Title: **Chosen of Tengri**
