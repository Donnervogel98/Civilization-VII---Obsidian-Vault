[[Diplomatic]], [[Expansionist]]

>*Chakravartin Ashoka, the wheel-turning monarch, your success in war made plain to you its costs. Determined to end the cycle of violence that marked your time as emperor of Maurya, you brought wisdom instead of swords, the dharma in place of destruction, and a message of non-violence to all beings. The world now needs your wisdom.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Dhammaraja*
- +1 Food in Cities for every 5 excess Happiness
- +10% Food in all Settlements during a Celebration
- Unique Buildings gain a +1 Happiness Adjacency for all Improvements

## Agenda
##### *Without Sorrow*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the highest Happiness yield
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the lowest Happiness yield

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Khmer]], [[Maurya]]
- Exploration: [[Chola]], [[Hawai'i]]
- Modern: [[Mughal]], [[Nepal]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Chakra***
	- +1 Food in the Capital for every 5 excess Happiness
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Gold & Sapphire Flowers***
	- Gain 100 Food in the Capital when you spend an Attribute Point on the Expansionist Attribute Tree
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Diamond Throne***
	- +1 Happiness per Age on Quarters during a Celebration
- Lvl 10 Title: **World Renouncer**
