[[Diplomatic]], [[Scientific]]

>*Himiko, shaman-queen of Yamatai, stands between worlds. As you bring the power of the land, you turn forests to shrines, waterfalls to music, and the mountains themselves to allies. The divine wind seeks to blow; great queen, direct its path.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Friend of Wei*
- Gain a unique Endeavor called **Friend of Wei** that can be performed in an Alliance to grant you and your ally +25% Science
- +4 Science per Age for every Leader you are Friendly or Helpful with
- Can support Endeavors for free, but Rejecting Diplomatic Actions costs +100% Influence

## Agenda
##### *Yamatai*
- ( - ) to a Leader for each of their Settlements with constructed Culture and Happiness Buildings
- ( + ) to a Leader for each of their Settlements with constructed Science and Gold Buildings

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Khmer]], [[Mississippian]], [[Tonga]]
- Exploration: [[Hawai'i]], [[Majapahit]]
- Modern: [[Meiji Japan]], [[Siam]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Golden Seal Stone***
	- +1 Influence per Age on Science Buildings
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Dōtaku***
	- Gain 50 Science when you spend an Attribute Point on the Scientific Attribute tree
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Yasakani no Magatama***
	- +2 Science per Age for every Technology Mastery completed
- Lvl 10 Title: **Queen of Wa**
