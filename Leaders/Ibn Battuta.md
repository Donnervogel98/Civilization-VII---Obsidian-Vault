[[Expansionist]], [[Wildcard]]

>*The wanderer's road is a long one, but there is no greater traveler than Muhammad ibn Battuta. The qadi gathers stories, resolves disputes, and compiles judgements for those who seek his wisdom. Take your pilgrim's staff in hand, and take the first step.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *The Marvels of Traveling*
- Gains 2 Wildcard Attribute Points after the first Civic in every Age
- +1 Sight for all Units
- Gain a unique Endeavor called Trade Maps that allows you to gradually see other Leaders' claimed territory

## Agenda
##### *Far and Wide*
- ( + + + ) to the Leader who has uncovered the most Fog of War tiles, or ( + + ) to each if there is a tie
- ( - ) to the Leader who has uncovered the fewest Fog of War tiles

## Starting Biases
- Flat

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Achaemenid Persia]], [[Aksum]], [[Carthage]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Songhai]]
- Modern: [[Buganda]], [[Mughal]], [[Ottomans]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
*The [[Wildcard]] Attribute grants atypical Attribute Nodes and Dedications*
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Traveller's Sandals***
	- Gain 10 of the corresponding yield whenever you spend an Attribute Point
- Lvl 3 Attribute Node: [[Economic]]
- Lvl 4 & Lvl 8 Dedications: [[Economic]], [[Cultural]]
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Merchant's Saddle***
	- +1 Movement for Scouts, Merchants, Settlers, and Explorers
- Lvl 7 Attribute Node: [[Expansionist]]
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***The Rihla***
	- +1 Culture, Gold, Happiness, and Science per turn per Age for each Attribute tree in which you have points
- Lvl 10 Title: **Marvelous Traveller**
