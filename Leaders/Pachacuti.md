[[Economic]], [[Expansionist]]

>*Sapa Inca Pachacuti, Earth-Shaker, your time has come. Bring your allies among the tirakuna, in the stones and light of the high places, in the print of the puma's paw – your people have need of you. You call to the mountains and they tremble for you. Let them tremble again.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *Earth Shaker*
- All Buildings receive a +1 Food adjacency for Mountains
- -2 Happiness Maintenance on Specialists adjacent to Mountains; +1 Happiness Maintenance on Specialists not adjacent to Mountains
- Cities gain a bonus to Production equal to 10% of that City's Food

## Agenda
##### *Mountain King*
- ( + + ) to the Leader with the fewest Mountains in their territory
- ( - - ) to the Leader with the most Mountains in their territory

## Starting Bias
- Mountains

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Maya]], [[Mississippian]]
- Exploration: [[Hawai'i]], [[Inca]]
- Modern: [[America]], [[Mexico]], [[Nepal]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Topayauri***
	- +1 Food per Age on Districts adjacent to Mountains
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***All-T'oqapu Tunic***
	- +1 Sight for Scouts adjacent to Mountains
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Mascapaycha***
	- +1 Gold and Food from Specialists in tiles adjacent to Mountains
- Lvl 10 Title: **Earth Shaker**
