## Leader Bonus
##### *Once Mortal, Twice Divine*
- +2 War Support when in only one Alliance
- When one of your Military Units defeats an enemy Unit, gain Influence equal to 50% of the defeated Unit's Combat Strength
- All basic Endeavors are automatically unlocked; Endeavors with Allies don't count against the limit of having one Endeavor of a given type at a time; Allies can support your Endeavors for free

## Agenda
##### *Ally of Enkidu*
- ( + + ) with Leaders who have started or supported the most Endeavors
- ( - ) with Leaders in Alliances with others

- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Magic Plant***
	- Friendly greetings give +100% relationship increase
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***Pukku***
	- +2 Happiness in all Settlements while you have exactly one Ally
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Mikku***
	- +1 Influence in all Settlements while you have exactly one Ally
- Lvl 10 Title: **Once Mortal, Twice Divine**
