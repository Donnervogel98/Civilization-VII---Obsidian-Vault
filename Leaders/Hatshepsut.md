[[Cultural]], [[Economic]]

>*Great Pharaoh Hatshepsut, your name is written again and again in immortal stone. Extend your hand now, and bring once again wonders from the sand. Ensure that your people, like the river, rise and fall, but never vanish.*

## Leader Bonus
##### *God's Wife of Amun*
- +1 Culture per Age for every unique Resource
- +15% Production towards constructing Buildings and Wonders in Cities adjacent to Navigable Rivers

## Agenda
##### *Wonders of Iteru*
- ( - ) to Leaders with more Wonders than Hatshepsut
- ( + + ) to Leaders with less Wonders than Hatshepsut

## Starting Biases
- Navigable Rivers
- Desert

## Recommended Civilizations
- Antiquity: [[Aksum]], [[Carthage]], [[Egypt]]
- Exploration: [[Abbasid]], [[Songhai]]
- Modern: [[Buganda]], [[Mughal]]

## Unique Legend Path Unlocks
- Lvl 2 Memento: ***Royal Mace***
	- +1 Gold per Age for each imported Resource
- Lvl 5 Memento: ***False Beard***
	- +2 Culture on Wonders
- Lvl 9 Memento: ***Uraeus***
	- +10 Culture per Age in Cities with at least 1 Wonder
- Lvl 10 Title: **Voice of the Sun**
